Site Plan

Name

I picked Silly Story Creator as the site name because I think it's unique and it will fit the content very well.

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to entertain users by asking them for nouns, verbs, adjectives etc. and populating short stories with the user-typed words.

Scenarios

1. What can I do to navigate to the original stories?

2. How can I submit my own story?
