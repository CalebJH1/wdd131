Home

Welcome to the home page of this website. In this website, you can click on "Story Creator" to list nouns, verbs, adjectives etc. to make silly stories, and the fun part is that you don't know what the story will be like until you start reading it. You can also go to the catalog to see the list of all stories, or if you want to create your very own story and submit it to the catalog, click on "Submit a Story" and you will be taken to a form to fill out. Have fun!
